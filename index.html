A true taste of India & Pakistan in Houston.

Welcome to Nirvana, a premier destination for authentic South Asian flavours and one of the most-loved Halal Indian restaurants in Houston. In the heart of Memorial Drive, we bring together traditional Indian and Pakistani recipes, freshly ground spices and warm hospitality — from slow-cooked curries and smoky tandoori to our celebrated halal Indian buffet.

Every dish is prepared with 100% halal-certified ingredients and made fresh to order, so each plate keeps its authentic taste, comforting aroma and consistent quality. Whether you're craving a flavourful curry, a sizzling tandoori grill, a spicy karahi or a complete Indian halal buffet, Nirvana is your go-to for family dining, celebrations and corporate gatherings.

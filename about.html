Our Story

A kitchen built on patience.

From a small family table in Amritsar to a candle-warm room on Memorial Drive — the same hands, the same spices, the same care.

Our dining room — designed to slow you down.

Since 2009

Three generations, one recipe book.

Nirvana began with a grandmother's handwritten recipes and a simple belief: that food made slowly, with whole spices and good company, tastes of home no matter how far you are from it.

When our family settled in Houston, we brought that book with us. Every curry on our menu traces back to it — adapted gently, never compromised. Today we serve thousands of guests a week, and we still grind our garam masala in small batches the way we always have.

We are proudly 100% Halal — not as a label, but as a promise to our community.

What guides us

Mission, vision & values.

Mission

To serve honest, deeply-flavoured Halal Indian food that makes every guest feel at home — and to do it consistently, every single day.

Vision

To be Houston's most trusted name in Halal Indian cuisine — the table families return to for ordinary Tuesdays and once-in-a-lifetime celebrations alike.

Values

Hospitality first. Spices whole and fresh. Halal without exception. Generosity at the table. And a calm room that respects your time.
